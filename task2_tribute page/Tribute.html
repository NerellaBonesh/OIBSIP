About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a Punjabi Hindu family. Virat Kohli is an Indian cricket player. Virat is one of the Indians favorite Indian players. He has so much fan following across the world. He is also known as RUN MACHINE because he can score runs in any format and against any team. Virat Kohli gets married to a Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11,2017. Their union was blessed with the arrival of a daughter in their lives. The couple named their first-born child as Vamika. In 2013, Kohli established the "Virat Kohli Foundation" with a philanthropic objective to support underprivileged children.

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020, Kohli and his wife Anushka Sharma pledged donations to the PM Relief Fund and the Maharashtra CM Relief Fund. The couple has a strong passion for animal welfare and have made personal contributions towards wildlife relief efforts. Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma have launched a new non-profit named SeVVA. The two have merged their respective foundations – Anushka Sharma Foundation and Virat Kohli Foundation - into the new NGO.
